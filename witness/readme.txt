== Description ==

Witnessai brings your site to life with header video and immersive featured images. With a focus on repair services and products sites, it features multiple sections on the front page as well as widgets, navigation and social menus, a logo, and more. Personalize its asymmetrical grid with a custom color scheme and showcase your multimedia content with post formats.
For more information about Witnessai please go to https://witnessai.wprealizer.com/
